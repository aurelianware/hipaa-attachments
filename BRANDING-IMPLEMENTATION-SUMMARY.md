## Branding Specifications Applied

### Visual Identity
- **Logo**: docs/images/logo-cloudhealthoffice-sentinel-primary.png.svg
- **Dimensions**: 800x400 pixels
- **Background**: Absolute black (#000000)
- **Primary Color**: Neon cyan (#00ffff)
- **Accent Color**: Neon green (#00ff88)

### Typography
- **Heading Font**: Segoe UI Bold
- **Body Font**: Segoe UI
- **Heading Color**: Neon cyan with glow effect
- **Body Color**: Light gray (#b0b0b0)

### Visual Effects
- **Circuit Veins**: Grid pattern overlay with cyan transparency
- **Glow Effects**: Text and box shadows with cyan/green
- **Borders**: 1-2px solid cyan or green

### Tone of Voice
- **Style**: Kubrickian, 2047-era
- **Characteristics**: Authoritative, inevitable, slightly ominous
- **Examples**:
  - "Just emerged from the void"
  - "The inevitable evolution"
  - "Capabilities beyond question"
  - "Systems that do not fail"
  - "Resistance is futile"
