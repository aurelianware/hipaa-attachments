**1.3 "Customer Data"** means all data, including Protected Health Information (PHI), that Customer submits to, or that is processed by, the Platform.

**1.4 "Documentation"** means the user manuals, technical specifications, and other documentation provided by Provider for the Platform.

**1.5 "Statement of Work" or "SOW"** means a written agreement executed by both Parties that describes specific services to be performed, deliverables, timelines, and pricing.

**1.6 "Authorized Users"** means Customer's employees, contractors, and agents who are authorized to access and use the Platform.

**1.7 "PHI"** has the meaning set forth in the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act of 1996 and its implementing regulations (collectively, "HIPAA").

## 5. TERM AND TERMINATION

**5.1 Term.** This Agreement commences on the Effective Date and continues for an initial term of twelve (12) months from the Platform go-live date ("Initial Term"), unless earlier terminated as provided herein. After the Initial Term, this Agreement will automatically renew for successive one (1) year terms ("Renewal Terms") unless either Party provides written notice of non-renewal at least thirty (30) days prior to the end of the then-current term.

**5.2 Termination for Convenience.** Either Party may terminate this Agreement for any reason upon thirty (30) days' written notice to the other Party.

**5.3 Termination for Cause.** Either Party may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice if the other Party:
- (a) Materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure within thirty (30) days of written notice;
- (b) Becomes insolvent or files for bankruptcy;
- (c) Ceases to operate in the normal course of business.

**5.4 Effect of Termination.**
- (a) Customer's license to the Platform terminates immediately;
- (b) Customer shall pay all outstanding Fees through the termination date;
- (c) Customer may export Customer Data for sixty (60) days following termination;
- (d) Provider will delete Customer Data within ninety (90) days following termination, except as required by law.

**5.5 Survival.** Sections 2.3 (Reservation of Rights), 4.4 (Taxes), 5.4 (Effect of Termination), 6 (Confidentiality), 7 (Data Security and HIPAA), 8 (Warranties), 9 (Indemnification), 10 (Limitation of Liability), and 11 (General Provisions) survive termination of this Agreement.

**11.3 Service Credits.** If Provider fails to meet the Availability Commitment, Customer is entitled to a service credit equal to:
- 99.0% - 99.8% availability: 10% of monthly Platform license fee
- 95.0% - 98.9% availability: 25% of monthly Platform license fee
- Below 95.0% availability: 50% of monthly Platform license fee

**11.4 Claiming Credits.** To receive service credits, Customer must request them within thirty (30) days of the end of the calendar month. Service credits are Customer's sole remedy for availability failures.

**11.5 Exclusions.** The Availability Commitment does not apply to unavailability caused by:
- (a) Customer's actions or systems;
- (b) Force majeure events;
- (c) Internet or third-party service provider failures;
- (d) Scheduled Maintenance;
- (e) Emergency maintenance to address security vulnerabilities.
