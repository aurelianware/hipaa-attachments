## Email Best Practices

### Subject Line Tips:
- Keep under 50 characters
- Include numbers/percentages (80%, $180K, etc.)
- Use action words (Reduce, Automate, Save)
- Avoid spam triggers (FREE, ACT NOW, LIMITED TIME)

### Body Copy Tips:
- Personalize with recipient's name and company
- Lead with pain points, not features
- Use bullet points for easy scanning
- Include specific numbers and ROI
- One clear call-to-action per email
- Keep under 200 words for cold emails

### Follow-Up Cadence:
- Day 1: Initial email
- Day 4: Follow-up #1
- Day 11: Follow-up #2 (case study)
- Day 25: Final follow-up (break-up email)
- Day 90: Re-engagement email

### A/B Testing Ideas:
- Test subject lines (pain point vs. benefit)
- Test email length (short vs. detailed)
- Test call-to-action (schedule demo vs. ask questions)
- Test send time (Tuesday 10am vs. Thursday 2pm)

### Metrics to Track:
- Open rate (target: >25%)
- Click-through rate (target: >5%)
- Reply rate (target: >2%)
- Demo conversion (target: >15% of replies)
- Proposal acceptance (target: >30% of demos)
